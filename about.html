Oxymoron Madrid is a new spanish brand of classic men shoes focusing on patterns and the use of new materials.

Why Oxymoron? Because it stands for the paradox that is represented through all the design concept:

Keeping the traditional shape of men lace up shoes but refreshing it with new materials, patterns and finishes. Classic but quirky!

We give special care to every detail aiming to provide a product that meets the highest standards of design and quality.

Made in Spain, as a seal of quality.
